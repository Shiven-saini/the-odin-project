Burger

Description

A burger is a cheesy combo of tikki sandwitched between two Ham Buns.

Ingredients

  • Potato Tikki
  • Burger-bun Breads
  • Butter
  • Salads
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Take 2 Burger Buns.
  2. Baske them lightly in some butter.
  3. Heat the aloo tikki and put it inside.
  4. Add some salads and sauce inside of it.
