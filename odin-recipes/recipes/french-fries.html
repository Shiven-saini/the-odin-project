French Fries

Description

French fries are deep-fried potatoes that are cut into thin strips and served hot and crispy. They are a popular snack or side dish that can be seasoned with salt, herbs, spices, or sauces.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel and wash the potatoes.
  2. Cut the potatoes in fine thin strips.
  3. Heat olive oil, enough to dip them.
  4. Soak them for atleast 5-6 min.
  5. Drain the oil out of them and wash inside cold water.
  6. Add salt for seasoning.
