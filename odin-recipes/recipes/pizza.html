Pizza

Description

A pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough.
  2. Let it rise.
  3. Add the toppings.
  4. Bake the pizza.
