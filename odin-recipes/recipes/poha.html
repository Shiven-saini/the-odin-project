Poha

Description

Poha is a popular Indian breakfast dish made from flattened rice, typically seasoned with turmeric and mustard seeds, and often garnished with peas, onions, and coriander.

Ingredients

  • Flattened rice
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salts
  • Chopped onions
  • Green chillies

Steps

  1. Prepare the poha.
  2. Add turmeric powder.
  3. Add baked peanuts and spicies.
  4. Garnish and serve.
